# HealthMesh AI Feature Audit

This document tracks the completion status of the core portals and features within the HealthMesh AI application.

## Patient Portal
- ✅ **Dashboard:** Central overview of health metrics and recent activity.
- ✅ **Vault:** Cryptographically sealed storage unit for medical records.
- ✅ **Consent Center:** Granular, time-bound consent management interface.
- ✅ **Audit Timeline:** Immutable log of all record access and system events.
- ✅ **Continuity Capsule:** AI-generated summary of longitudinal health data.
- ✅ **Emergency Capsule:** Break-glass summary for first responders.
- ✅ **Delegates:** Management of authorized family members or caregivers.
- ✅ **Profile:** Standard demographic and contact information.

## Hospital Portal
- ✅ **Search:** Global patient index search for authorized providers.
- ✅ **Access Requests:** Interface to request scope-limited consent from patients.
- ✅ **Prescriptions:** Digital prescribing with AI conflict checking.
- ✅ **Break Glass:** Emergency access protocol bypassing standard consent.

## Pharmacy Portal
- ✅ **QR Verification:** Cryptographic scanning of digital prescriptions.
- ✅ **Dispensing:** Workflow for fulfilling and logging medication dispenses.
- ✅ **Fraud Detection:** AI-powered anomaly detection for prescription fraud (e.g., cross-state duplication).

## Insurance Portal
- ✅ **Claims:** Overview of active and historical claims tied to cryptographic proofs.
- ✅ **Verification:** Automated verification of procedures against active policy scopes.

## AI Intelligence Center
- ✅ **Safety Checks:** Real-time drug interaction and allergy contraindication checks.
- ✅ **Health Vector:** Multi-dimensional visualization of patient health trajectories.
- ✅ **Risk Analysis:** Predictive modeling for readmission and complication risks.
- ✅ **Continuity Generation:** Automated synthesis of fragmented records into cohesive narratives.

## Audit Center
- ✅ **Logs:** Raw system logs detailing every authentication and access attempt.
- ✅ **Events:** Semantic categorization of system events.
- ✅ **Search:** Filtering and querying interface for compliance officers.

*(Note: While the UI and navigational structure for these features are marked as complete (✅), full backend enforcement and data persistence are tracked in the `PROJECT_STATUS.md` report).*
